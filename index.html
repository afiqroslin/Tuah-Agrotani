About Us

Tuah Agrotani Sdn Bhd is a supplier of fresh vegetable crops committed to quality and freshness from local farms. We also provide systematic packaging, storage, and wholesale distribution services to ensure products reach customers in optimal condition. With an efficient supply chain, we support the needs of retailers, wholesalers and business partners nationwide.

Vision

To become a leading supplier of fresh, high-quality agricultural products by setting standards of sustainability and excellence in the industry. We aim to create a world where nutritious food is readily accessible, enjoyable, and beneficial to both individuals and the environment. We also encourage consumers to support local farmers in this sector.

Mission

Maintain strict quality and freshness standards throughout the supply chain. We provide after‑sales support to maximize customer satisfaction. We emphasize building long‑term partnerships with suppliers and integrating farms into our production cycle.
